Short bio

I am PhD student at the UT Autonomous group since August 2015. I received my M.Sc. in Aerospace Engineering in June 2017 at the University of Texas at Austin. I received my B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering in June 2015 at the Bogazici University, Turkey.

My research is on the theoretical and algorithmic aspects of the design and verification of autonomous systems. My research interests are verification and synthesis in uncertain and dynamic environments, synthesis of heterogeneous multi-agent systems, and designing human-autonomy systems with realistic cognitive models.

Research Interests

  • Verification and synthesis of uncertain and parametric probabilistic systems

  • Distributed synthesis of heterogeneous multi-agent systems

  • Verifiable human-robot interaction

  • Formal methods and control theory for robotics applications

  • Autonomy and cyber-physical systems

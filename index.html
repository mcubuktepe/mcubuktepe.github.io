Short bio

I am PhD student at the UT Autonomous group since August 2015. I received my M.Sc. in Aerospace Engineering in June 2017 at the University of Texas at Austin. I received my B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering in June 2015 at the Bogazici University, Turkey.

My research focuses on developing theory and algorithms that guarantee desired behavior of autonomous systems operating in uncertain and partially observable environments. I apply tools from formal methods, control and optimization to develop scalable methods to guarantee the correct behavior of autonomous systems.

Research Interests

  • Verification and synthesis of uncertain and parametric probabilistic systems

  • Convex optimization applications in formal methods, learning, robotics and control

  • Partially observable probabilistic systems, like POMDPs and POSGs

  • Autonomy and Cyber-physical systems
